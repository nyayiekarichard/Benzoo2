Help & Support

Welcome to the help page of Benz's By Richie. Below you can find answers to common questions and guidance on how to use the site.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How do I list my car for sale?

To list your car for sale, navigate to the Sell Your Car page. Fill out the form with details about your car, including make, model, year, price, and upload a photo. Once you submit the form, your car will be added to our listings.

How can I edit or remove my car listing?

To edit or remove your car listing, go to the Sell Your Car page. You'll see your car listings there with options to edit or delete them.

What if I need financing?

We offer financing options for eligible buyers. Visit our Financing page to learn more and apply for a car loan.

How do I contact customer support?

If you need further assistance, you can contact our support team via the Contact page. We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours.
